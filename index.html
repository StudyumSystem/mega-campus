About the Summit

Governance & Democracy

Prototype governance frameworks inspired by ancient democracies, scaled through blockchain infrastructure and zero-knowledge cryptography.

Web3 & Network States

Explore decentralized accelerationism, public goods funding, and the future of digital coordination at the intersection of tech and society.

AI & Autonomous Agents

Dive into artificial intelligence, autonomous systems, and how AI intersects with decentralized decision-making and governance.

Regenerative Finance

Explore ReFi, decentralized physical infrastructure networks, and how blockchain can power regenerative economic systems.
